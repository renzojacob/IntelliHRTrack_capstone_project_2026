Quick Help

How is the limit counted?

1 leave request = 1 count, kahit ilang days ang sakop. Drafts are not counted until submitted.

Can I cancel a request?

Yes, you can cancel only when status is PENDING.

When does it reset?

It resets every new year automatically.
