Formula Guide

How Your Payroll Is Computed

Rates

Daily Rate = Monthly Salary / Salary Divisor

Hourly Rate = Daily Rate / 8

Per Minute

Per-Minute Rate = Hourly Rate / 60

Used for late and undertime deductions.

JO

Job Order follows no work, no pay based on actual worked days.

COS

COS uses salary rules. Valid holidays and suspensions are not deducted.
